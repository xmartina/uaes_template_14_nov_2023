ADMISSION INTO DEGREE PROGRAMMES OF THE UNIVERSITY OF AGRICULTURE AND ENVIRONMENTAL SCIENCES, UMUAGWO, FOR 2022/2023 SESSION

Applications are invited from suitably qualified candidates for admission into the following fulltime programmes of the University of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences, Umuagwo, Imo State, Nigeria.

  1. PROGRAMMES:
    1. FACULTY  OF AGRICULTURE 

Courses available

  1.       B. Agric.  in
  1. Agricultural Extension
  2. Agricultural Economics
  3. Animal Production
  4. Crop Production
  5. Soil Science
  1.        B.Aqua./Fisheries
  1. Fisheries & Aquaculture
  1.       B.Sc. in
  1. Food Science and Technology

 

    1. FACULTY  OF SCIENCE AND COMPUTING (FSC)

Courses available

  1. Biology
  2. Microbiology
  3. Biotechnology
  4. Biochemistry
  5. Mathematics
  6. Computer Science
  7. Information Technology
  8. Physics
  9. Chemistry

  5. Chemical and Petroleum Engineering

 

  1. GENERAL ENTRY REQUIREMENTS FOR DEGREE PROGRAMME    

This admission is open for candidates who sat for the UTME in 2022/2023 session and scored a minimum of 140. 

Candidates who did not choose UAES as first choice University but scored the required cut-off mark can also apply on the condition that they visit the JAMB website for a change of Institution.

All candidates must have five (5) O’ level credits in relevant subjects at not more than two (2) sittings. Candidates waiting O’ level results can also apply but they will not be considered for admission until their results are available. 

 

  1. HOW TO APPLY (UAES)
  1. Visit the university website at: www.uaes.edu.ng and click on admissions on the top menu of the homepage
  2. You will be directed to the admission section of the portal, under 2022/2023 Post UTME/Direct Entry Application, click on “click here to apply”
  3. Signup with your jamb registration number, email, phone number and password
  4. Verify your email from your email account and return to the portal to pay the application fee of N2000
  5. Pay with your card online or generate the reference number and go to any bank branch of your choice to pay physically at the bank, and then come back to continue with your application.
  6. If you make the payment successfully, you would be asked to click to continue your application and at this point, you will access the application form
  7. Fill the form completely and correctly and then click submit at the bottom of the form.
  8. If you were unable to complete your application for any reason,
  9. you can always come back to the admission portal at: https://admissions.uaes.edu.ng  to login to your account and complete your application before the closing date: __________________________________________________________
  10. After submitting your application form, print the acknowledgement slip and come with it to the university on the Post UTME/Direct Entry screening day to be announced by the university subsequently

The University of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences (UAES) Umuagwo, is a public University established by the act of Imo State Parliament Law No. 4 of 2019. The establishment of this University was a direct response of the Government of Imo State to the burning need for more credible tertiary Institutions needed to address the yearnings of the teeming youths of Imo State and the broader South-Eastern Nigeria desiring for qualitative third level education.
