Admission Requirements for 2023/2024

Prospective students must, in addition to meeting the general admission requirements, also satisfy the school and departmental requirements detailed below:

 

 

  1. Faculty of Agriculture

Programme

 

Admission Requirements

Remarks

UTME

Direct Entry

B. Agriculture: 

(i) Agric. Economics,

(ii) Agric Extension, 

(iii) Crop production,

(iv) Animal production, 

(v) Soil Science

 

(vi) Forestry & Wildfire 

Five Credit passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English, Mathematics, Chemistry and Biology/ Agricultural Science. At least a pass in Physics. At least a pass in Biology must back up the Credit pass in Agric Science.

ND/HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in relevant field or Two ‘A’ level passes or JUPEB passes in Chemistry and one of Botany/Zoology/ Biology/ Physics/Agric. Science, Geography, Maths., Geology, Economics and Health Science.

UTME subjects are English language, Chemistry, Biology/ Agricultural Science and Mathematics/ Physics.

B. Fisheries and Aquaculture

 

 

 

 

Five Credit passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English, Mathematics, Chemistry and Biology/ Agricultural Science. At least a pass in Physics. At least a pass in Biology must back up the Credit pass in Agric Science.

ND/HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in relevant field or Two ‘A’ level passes or JUPEB passes in Chemistry and one of Botany/Zoology/ Biology/ Physics/Agric. Science, Geography, Maths., Geology, Economics and Health Science.

UTME subjects are English Language, Chemistry, Biology/ Agricultural Science and Mathematics/ Physics.

 

  1. Faculty of Engineering

Admission Requirements

Direct Entry

B.Eng.  

(i) Civil Engineering   

(ii) Water and Irrigation Engineering 

Five Credit level passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English Language, Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry and one of Further Mathematics, Technical Drawing or any other Science subject.

ND/HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in relevant field, Two ‘A’ level passes, or JUPEB passes in Chemistry, Physics or Mathematics.

UTME subjects are English language, Mathematics, Chemistry and Physics.

B.Eng.                 

(i) Mechanical Engineering,        

(ii) Electrical and Electronics Engineering,  

(iii) Chemical and Petroleum Engineering

Five Credit level passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English Language, Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry and one of Further Mathematics, Technical Drawing or any other Science subject.

ND/HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in relevant field, Two ‘A’ level passes, or JUPEB passes in Chemistry, Physics or Mathematics.

UTME subjects are English language, Mathematics, Chemistry and Physics.

 

  1. Faculty of Environmental Sciences

Programme

Admission Requirements

Remarks

 

UTME

Direct Entry

 

B.Sc. Environmental Management & Toxicology.

 

Five Credit level passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English Language, Mathematics, Chemistry and Biology/Agric. Science and any other Science subjects in not more than two sittings. Candidates must also have at-least a pass in physics.

ND/HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in relevant field or Two ‘A’ level passes or JUPEB passes (or equivalent) in Biology and Chemistry, 

 

 

UTME subjects are: English language, Chemistry, Physics/Mathematics, Biology or any relevant Science subject.

B.Sc. Estate Management and Valuation

Five Credit level passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English Language, Mathematics, Economics and any other two subjects from Chemistry, Physics, Business Studies/ Commerce, Geography, Biology, Agricultural Science and Technical Drawing in not more than two sittings. 

ND/HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass  in Estate Management or allied professions or professional certificate may be assessed and placed appropriately or ‘A’ level passes or JUPEB passes (or equivalent) in two relevant subjects.

UTME subjects are English language, and any three of the following; Economics, Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry, Geography, Biology, Agricultural Science. 

B.Sc. Horticulture & Landscape Architecture

Five Credit level passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English Language, Mathematics, Biology and two other subjects from the following list: Chemistry, Physics, Technical Drawing, Fine Arts, Geography, Economics, Building Construction, Land Surveying and Agriculture in not more than two sittings.

ND/ HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in Architecture, Urban Planning, Building, Estate Management, Land Survey or Fine Arts  or ‘A’ level passes or JUPEB passes (or equivalent) in Biology and Physics, or Mathematics or Geography.

UTME subjects are: English language, Biology and any two of the following: Economics, Physics, Mathematics, Geography, Chemistry,

 

  1. Faculty of Arts, Social and Management Sciences

Programme

Admission Requirements

Remarks

UTME

Direct Entry

B.Sc  Criminology & Security Studies 

Five Credit level passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English Language, Mathematics, either Economics, Geography or Science and other two subjects, in not more than two sittings.

 

ND/ HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in relevant field, or Candidates shall possess five credit passes in the SSCE (or equivalents) of which two shall be at advanced level (or JUPEB), or four credits passes of which at least three shall be at advanced level (or JUPEB) provided that such passes are not counted at both levels of the examinations. For emphasis, credit in English Language, Mathematics and any of Economics, Geography or Government is compulsory.

UTME subjects are: English language, History/ Government and any other two subjects from Arts and Social Sciences.

B.Sc. Peace Studies & Conflict Resolution.

 

Five Credit level passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English Language, Mathematics and Government in not more than two sittings.

 

ND/ HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in relevant field, or Candidates shall possess five credit passes in the SSCE (or equivalents) of which two shall be at advanced level (or JUPEB), provided that such passes are not counted at both levels of the examinations. For emphasis, credit in English Language, Mathematics and Economics are required..

UTME subjects are: English language, History/ Government and any other two subjects from Arts and Social Sciences.

B.Sc. Psychology

Five Credit level passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English Language, Mathematics and Economics in not more than two sittings.

ND/ HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in relevant field, or Candidates shall possess five credit passes in the SSCE (or equivalents) of which two shall be at advanced level (or JUPEB), provided that such passes are not counted at both levels of the examinations. For emphasis, credit in English Language, Mathematics and Economics are required..

UTME subjects are: English language, Economics and any other two subjects from Arts and Social Sciences.

B.Sc. (i) Accounting,  (ii) Taxation,        (iii) Business Administration,     (iv) Cooperative and Rural Development, (v) Marketing,      (vi) Entrepreneurship 

Five Credit level passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English Language, Mathematics and Economics in not more than two sittings

ND/HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in relevant discipline or Final Certificate of relevant Professional Bodies or Candidates shall possess five credit passes in the SSCE (or equivalents) of which two shall be at advanced level (or JUPEB), or four credits passes of which at least three shall be at advanced level (or JUPEB) provided that such passes are not counted at both levels of the examinations. Credit passes at Ordinary level must include English Language and Mathematics.

UTME subjects are: English language, Mathematics Economics and any Social Science subject.

 

  1. Faculty of Science and Computing

Programme

Admission Requirements

Remarks

UTME

Direct Entry

B.Sc.  (i) Chemistry, 

(ii) Computer Science, 

(iii) Information Technology, 

(iv) Mathematics, (v) Physics. 

Five Credit level passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English Language, Mathematics, Chemistry Physics and any other relevant science subjects in not more than two sittings.

ND/ HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in relevant field or Two ‘A’ level passes (or JUPEB passes) in two relevant subjects (Biology, Botany, Chemistry, Geography, Mathematics and Physics) 

UTME subjects are: English Language, Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry

B.Sc. (i) Biology, 

 

(ii) Biochemistry

(iii) Biotechnology,

(iv) Microbiology,

Five Credit level passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English Language, Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology and any other     relevant science subjects     Technical Drawing, Agricultural Science and Geography and at least a pass in Physics in not more than two sittings.

ND/ HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in relevant field or Two ‘A’ level passes (or JUPEB passes) in two relevant subjects (Biology, Botany, Chemistry, Geography, Mathematics and Physics)

UTME subjects are: English Language, Biology, Chemistry and any relevant science subject.

B.Sc. Food Science and Technology

Five Credit passes at SSCE (or its equivalent) including English Language, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and any other Science subject.

ND/HND with minimum of Lower Credit level pass in relevant field or Two ‘A’ level passes (or JUPEB passes) in Chemistry, Physics or Mathematics. 

UTME subjects are: English language, Chemistry, Biology,Physics or Mathematics. 

 

The University of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences (UAES) Umuagwo, is a public University established by the act of Imo State Parliament Law No. 4 of 2019. The establishment of this University was a direct response of the Government of Imo State to the burning need for more credible tertiary Institutions needed to address the yearnings of the teeming youths of Imo State and the broader South-Eastern Nigeria desiring for qualitative third level education.
