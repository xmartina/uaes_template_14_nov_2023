PROF. CHRISTOPHER C. EZE, JP.

B.Agric. (Hons) MSc., PhD(Nig) L.L.B., B.L., Dip. in SMEs, FNAAE,FFAMAN,FASN,EMF

Ag. VICE CHANCELLOR

WELCOME TO

UAES, UMUAGWO

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to University of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences (UAES). UAES was established in 2019 with the vision of pursuing the advancement of learning and academic excellence, the university has been unrelenting in the pursuit of its mission of becoming a citadel of learning, a community with the trademark of excellence in teaching, research and service to humanity, a catalyst as well as an agent for development.

On my resumption as the Acting Vice Chancellor of this great institution I brought a unique innovation to bear on the mandate of the university. I have mandated every staff and students to donate, plant and nurture a fruit bearing tree with a view to greeing the university and creating a sustainable environment. Our collective interest in trasforming our Nation must be encouraged, hence I am inviting everyone interested in this transformation which UAES is championing to join us in creating sustainability in terms of knowledge, practice and leadership.

The University of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences (UAES) Umuagwo, is a public University established by the act of Imo State Parliament Law No. 4 of 2019. The establishment of this University was a direct response of the Government of Imo State to the burning need for more credible tertiary Institutions needed to address the yearnings of the teeming youths of Imo State and the broader South-Eastern Nigeria desiring for qualitative third level education.
